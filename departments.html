Our Departments

LifeCare Hospital offers a wide range of medical departments dedicated to delivering comprehensive healthcare. Each department is staffed by highly qualified doctors and equipped with modern facilities to ensure the best care.

Cardiology

Our cardiology department provides advanced cardiac care and diagnostics, including ECG, angiography, and heart surgeries handled by expert cardiologists.

Neurology

Specializing in treating brain and nervous system disorders, our neurology team offers 24x7 emergency neuro services and advanced imaging facilities.

Orthopedics

The orthopedics department provides treatment for bone fractures, joint issues, arthritis, and sports injuries with both surgical and non-surgical approaches.

Pediatrics

Our pediatrics unit offers specialized care for children, from newborns to adolescents, focusing on growth, development, and preventive health.

Gynecology

The gynecology and obstetrics department provides maternity care, women’s wellness programs, and safe childbirth services.

Emergency & Trauma Care

Our emergency department operates 24x7, equipped with rapid response teams, modern ambulances, and advanced life-support systems.
